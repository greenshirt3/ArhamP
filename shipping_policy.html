Shipping and Order Placement Policy

Easy Guide to Ordering, Production, and Delivery

1. Placing Your Order (Proof & Payment)

For custom printing, our process is designed to prevent mistakes since the products cannot be resold.

Step Requirement Why this is critical
Proof Approval is Final You must review the digital proof (spelling, size, placement) and formally approve it before we print. You are responsible for any errors (like a typo or a wrong phone number) that were visible on the final proof you approved before printing. We are not liable for these mistakes after your approval.
Payment Upfront We require **100% payment upfront** (Cash with Order) to start production. Because every product is custom-made and cannot be resold, we cannot accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for custom print jobs.
Color Disclaimer The colors you see on your computer screen or mobile phone will **not perfectly match** the final printed product. Monitor colors (RGB) are different from printing ink colors (CMYK). By approving the proof, you accept this standard color variance.

2. Production & Timelines

Your total wait time is the **Production Time** (our work) PLUS the **Shipping Time** (courier's work).

Timeline Details Critical Information
**Production Time (TAT)** This is the time we need to print, cut, and finish your order (e.g., 5-7 Business Days).
The clock starts **ONLY** when we have both your **100% Payment AND Final Proof Approval**.
**Shipping Time (Courier)** This is the time the courier takes to deliver (e.g., 1-3 Business Days).
This begins once the order leaves our shop.

3. Getting Your Order

You have two options for receiving your printed goods:

Local Pickup

Collect your order directly from our Jalalpur Jattan shop. We will notify you when it is ready. Please bring your Order ID and CNIC for verification.

Domestic Shipping

We use reliable national couriers (like TCS or CallCourier) for delivery across Pakistan. Shipping fees are calculated by the courier based on weight and distance, and will be shown on your final invoice.

**Important Note on Shipping Risk:** Once the package is handed over to the courier, **we are not responsible for delays, loss, or damage during transit.** We strongly recommend purchasing Shipment Insurance (CVI) for high-value orders to protect your investment.
