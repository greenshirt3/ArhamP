Return and Refund Policy

(Applicable to Custom-Printed Goods and Services)

Our commitment is to provide high-quality custom printing. Because all our products are personalized, they are generally **Final Sale**. Please review your digital proofs and order details carefully before confirming your purchase.

1. All Sales Are Final (Non-Returnable Items)

Custom-printed products are strictly non-returnable and non-refundable. This includes all paper-based products (such as business cards, invitations, brochures, banners, etc.) that have been printed with your unique text, images, or design.

We cannot accept returns or issue refunds for:

  • **Buyerâ€™s Remorse:** Change of mind, accidental orders, or if you found a better price elsewhere.
  • **Customer Errors:** Mistakes in spelling, punctuation, grammar, or design that were present in the final proof you approved before printing.
  • **Quantity/Size:** Ordering the wrong size or quantity.

2. Exceptions (Shop Responsibility)

You are eligible for a **replacement or store credit** only if the item is defective due to a proven manufacturing or printing error on our part. This includes:

  • **Product Damage:** The product is damaged, torn, or defective upon collection or delivery.
  • **Printing Error:** The final printed product significantly deviates from the approved design proof (e.g., wrong stock or incorrect color profile used), and the mistake was not present in the files you provided or approved.

3. How to Report an Issue

If your order falls under the exceptions listed in Section 2:

  • **Time Limit:** You must notify our Jalalpur Jattan shop within **48 hours (2 days)** of collecting or receiving your order.
  • **Required Documentation:** You must provide the original receipt (Proof of Purchase) and the full order of faulty goods for inspection.

Report an Issue Now

4. Resolution

For all approved claims under Section 2, our primary resolution will be:

  • **Replacement/Reprint:** We will promptly re-print and replace the defective portion of your order at no additional cost.
  • **Store Credit/Refund:** A monetary refund will only be issued if replacement or exchange is not possible.
